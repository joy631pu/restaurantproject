RELAY FOR LIFE

Whether you are cheering for survivors, passing the baton to your teammate, or joining in the exciting trackside activities, Relay For Life is more than a cancer walk – it’s a journey. In support of the Canadian Cancer Society, Relay For Life spans the entire country with events in over 330 communities nationwide and last year alone raised over $34,000,000 for the cause. To show our support, our Comox Valley location is donating burgers to fuel the participants of the Mark R. Isfeld Senior Secondary School on December 10th.
