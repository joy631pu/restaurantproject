Project Proposal

I am going to develop a website for a restaurant named "Delicious Inn" which is a new restaurant situated in Comox,BC.

Project Goals

  • Functionality: My first goal is to ensure the functionality of my project as without proper function, a website is useless.
  • Accessibility: I will ensure that all of my website pages will be easily accessible by our clients.
  • Responsiveness: My website will be fully responsive regardless of the screen sizes of different devices.
  • Generate Traffic for Clients: I will implement a good SEO while developing my project in order to generate a good number of traffic for the restaurant.

Project Features

  • Home Page: By using my home page, I will present an attractive interface to the viewers. The viewers will be able to get as much information as they need, regarding "Delicious Inn".
  • Menu Page: This page will provide all of the food items which are available in the restaurant based on the viewers selected category.
  • Service Page:Visitors will be able to know which type of services like: Dine In, Drive Through, Online Order are available in the restaurant.
  • About Page: It will provide all of the information regarding "Delicious Food" that means the restaurant will introduce itself by using this "About" page.
  • Contact Page:People will get restaurant's address, contact number, e-mail address on this page together with they can provide their own information as well as any complain by using this page.

Project Developing Methodology

Agile Methodology

I would like to follow "Agile Methodology" for developing my project because this method is collaborative and open to data-driven change. Besides, agile project management methodologies usually involve short phases of work with frequent testing, reassessment, and adaptation throughout which are my preferred steps of work.
