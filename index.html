Welcome to Delicious Inn

Through 20 years of innovation Delicious Inn has developed Fast-food recipes that reflect consumer trends. Not limited to only Pizzas, Delicious Inn has ventured to new sandwich frontiers, challenging norms and exciting Canadian test buds across the country.

Our Services

Healthy Foods

Our clean label commitment means nothing is added to your meat but simple, delicious ingredients you can pronounce like: 'organic tomatoes'

Sustainable Boxes

Do you know our Pizza boxes are made with Foresty Stewardship Council certified cardboard? Each year, this commitment to sustainably sourced fiber helps us reduce our footprint in a big way.

Original Recipes

With our animal-based proteins, we serve products that are of the highest quality and are working to ensure that all of our meat comes from animals that are raised humanely.
